Wei Chen is an Associate Professor of Operations and Information Management (OPIM) at the School of Business, University of Connecticut. He is also the academic director of the UConn Digital Frontiers Initiative, which aims to facilitate collaborations between industry and academia, focusing on cutting edge technology developments, such as generative AI.

Wei's research studies platforms & crowds, financial technologies (FinTech), and most recently, generative AI design and governance. Combining theoretical models and granular data, he seeks to provide rigorous evidence that informs the system and market design in these areas. His work has been published in leading academic journals, such as Management Science, Information Systems Research, and MIS Quarterly.
